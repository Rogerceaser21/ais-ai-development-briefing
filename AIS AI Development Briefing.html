03 A problem worth fixing

Automating cover exposed a data problem.

To put cover on the right calendars, every teacher record had to be correct. It wasn't.

~3,000 vs ~400"active" directory accounts versus real cover-eligible staff
47records carrying someone else's email address
67staff rows with no surname recorded
0reconciliation between HR, timetabling and IT

Durable fixes are being routed back to source with Pic, Boyd and IT. The data, not just the automation, is the win.

04 The Feedback App

The teacher's feedback, not a generic AI's.

Tools like Brisk and Chalkie auto-write student feedback. Parents want the teacher's own expertise, and unchecked AI errors slip through to children.

This app keeps the teacher as the author: they record real feedback in their own voice, screen or video, stored so students can replay it anytime, until they understand.

Dave and I rank this our top priority after the dashboard and reports.
